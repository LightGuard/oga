Style of hotel and ambience
Sleek, modern, artistic - enter the Dutch Design Hotel Artemis and be transported to an engaging realm of style and design. Each stylish room comes with a host of facilities that guarantee a pleasant stay. In the pleasant atmosphere of the restaurant, culinary treats court the hearts and palates of the guests. Understanding the needs of the discerning business travellers, the hotel features a variety of conference rooms.
Special features of the hotel
On eight floors you find 256 comfortable rooms, 11 multi-funtional meetingrooms, Restaurant-Bar 'De Stijl' and a large water-side terrace.

Conveniently situated in Amsterdam, the hotel provides an ideal location for both private and business travellers. The city centre is reachable within a few minutes by car and allures with famous sights such as the Rijksmuseum, the Van Gogh Museum and the Stedelijk Museum. Amsterdam Airport is located at a distance of 9 kilometres.
